bacon and egg tacos

Ingredients
3 eggs
2 slices bacon
2 tortillas
1 pinch salt
1 pinch pepper

directions
Scramble the eggs in a pot or skillet. Adding salt and pepper as desired.
Put the eggs in a bowl or plate and wipe out the skillet or pan.
Cook the bacon slices, if there is pooling grease drain off.
Plate by warming the tortillas up then measuring out half the egg into
each tortilla. Then lay or break up one slice bacon per tortilla.
